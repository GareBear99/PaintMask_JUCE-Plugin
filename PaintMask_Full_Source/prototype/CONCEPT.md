# PaintMask — Canonical Product Concept

PaintMask is a visual sequencer/instrument where:
- X axis = time
- Y axis = pitch
- color = sound behavior
- opacity = gate/probability feel
- thickness = density/length feel

## Lite
- Brush
- Splash
- Line
- Mask
- Left-to-right / ping-pong scan
- Preview synth
- MIDI concept direction

## Studio
- Star / spiral / motion brushes
- circular / spiral scan modes
- future modulation layers
- geometry packs
- export packs
