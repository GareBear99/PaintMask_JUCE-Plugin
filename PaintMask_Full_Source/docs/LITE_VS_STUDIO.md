# Lite vs Studio

## Lite
- Brush
- Splash
- Line
- Mask
- RGB color mapping
- Left-to-right scan
- Preview synth
- MIDI out
- Save/load

## Studio
- Star, Spiral, Harmony, Motion tools
- Circular, Spiral, Ping-Pong, Random Walk scans
- Modulation paint layers
- Export helpers
- Theme packs
- Future premium brush packs

## Trial policy
- First 30 days = full Studio
- After expiry = automatic Lite fallback
- Subscription = $1/month to restore Studio
